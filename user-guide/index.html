Getting Started

Creating Your Account

  1. Download the App

    Available on iOS App Store and Google Play Store. Search for "Dinkli" or visit dinkli.com

  2. Sign Up

    Open the app and tap "Create New Account". Enter your email address and create a password. Verify your email address (check your inbox).

  3. Complete Your Profile

    Select your skill level (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced, or Open). Add your name and phone number. Optionally add a profile picture. Choose your membership plan (Drop-in or Monthly).

  4. Start Exploring

    Tap "Find Games" to browse available games. Filter by skill level if desired. Book your first game!

Guest Mode: You can browse games without creating an account by tapping "Browse Games Without Account" on the login screen. However, you'll need to sign in to book games.

Booking Games

How to Book a Game

  1. Browse Games

    Go to the Explore tab. Scroll through available games by day. Use the skill level filter if needed.

  2. Select a Game

    Tap on a game card to view details. Check the time, location, and skill level. Review the number of spots available.

  3. Reserve Your Spot

    Tap "Reserve Spot" button. If you have Drop-in membership: Confirm payment ($10). If you have Monthly membership: Booking is free. You'll receive a confirmation.

  4. Confirmation

    Game appears in your "Upcoming Games" on the Home tab. You'll see it in your Profile ‚Üí Bookings. Set a reminder for yourself!

Canceling a Booking

  1. Find Your Booking

    Go to Home tab ‚Üí Upcoming Games, OR go to Profile ‚Üí View your bookings, OR go to the game details page.

  2. Cancel

    Tap on the game. Tap "Cancel Registration". Confirm cancellation.

  3. Refund

    If canceled 24+ hours before: Full refund (5-7 business days). If canceled less than 24 hours: Refund may be forfeited. Monthly members: No refund needed (no charge per game).

Full Games: If a game shows "Full" (e.g., "6 of 6"), no more spots are available. Check back later in case someone cancels. Book early for popular games!

Weekly Game Reset

What is it?

Every Sunday at 6:00 AM Central Time, past games are automatically removed. New games for the upcoming week are created from fixed game templates. This keeps the app clean and organized.

What This Means for You:

  • Past games disappear from the app (they're archived)
  • New week's games appear automatically
  • Your bookings are preserved
  • No action needed from you
