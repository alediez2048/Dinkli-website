Frequently Asked Questions

What equipment do I need to bring?
Nothing! Game captains provide all equipment including nets, rackets, and balls. Just show up ready to play.
Where are the games played?
Games are organized on outdoor public courts - basketball courts, football fields, tennis courts, or any concrete surface. We use public spaces to keep costs low.
How do I know my skill level?
Beginner: New to pickleball or playing for less than 6 months. Intermediate: Regular player with 6+ months experience. Advanced: Competitive player with strong skills.
Can I cancel my membership?
Yes! You can cancel your monthly membership at any time. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees.
What if a game is cancelled?
If a game is cancelled due to weather or other reasons, you'll receive a full refund or credit for a future game.
How do I become a game captain?
Game captains are experienced players who organize games. Contact us through the app if you're interested in becoming a captain!
