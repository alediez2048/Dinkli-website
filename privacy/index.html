Privacy Policy for Dinkli

Effective Date: December 3, 2025
Last Updated: December 3, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Dinkli ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide

When you create an account and use Dinkli, we collect:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth
  • Profile Information: Display name, skill level, playing experience, preferred play style, profile picture
  • Location Information: City, state, zip code (for finding nearby games)
  • Payment Information: Payment method details (processed securely through Stripe)

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Games viewed, games booked, app interactions
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version
  • Performance Data: Crash reports, error logs (for app improvement)

Photos and Camera

  • Camera: Used to take profile pictures
  • Photo Library: Used to select existing photos for your profile
  • Purpose: Profile personalization only
  • Optional: You can use the app without providing a profile picture

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Provide Services: Enable game booking, profile management, and core app features
  • Process Payments: Handle transactions securely through Stripe
  • Send Notifications: Game reminders, booking confirmations, and important updates
  • Improve Experience: Analyze usage patterns to enhance app functionality
  • Communicate: Send account-related information and customer support
  • Match Players: Connect you with games matching your skill level and location

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We share data with trusted third-party services:

Supabase (Backend Services)

  • What We Share: Account data, game data, booking information
  • Purpose: Database storage and backend infrastructure
  • Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

Stripe (Payment Processing)

  • What We Share: Payment method information, transaction data
  • Purpose: Secure payment processing
  • Privacy Policy: https://stripe.com/privacy

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
  • Authentication: Secure login with email verification
  • Access Control: Role-based permissions and Row-Level Security (RLS)
  • Regular Audits: Security reviews and updates

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View your personal data
  • Correct: Update inaccurate information
  • Delete: Request account and data deletion
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
  • Export: Download your data

To exercise these rights: Contact us at privacy@dinkli.com

Data Retention

We retain your data:

  • Active Accounts: As long as your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: 30 days after deletion (for recovery)
  • Legal Requirements: As required by law
  • Game History: Archived for record-keeping (7 days)

Children's Privacy

Dinkli is intended for users aged 12 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 12. If we discover we have collected data from a child under 12, we will delete it immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the app and on our website. Continued use of Dinkli after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our data practices:

  • Email: privacy@dinkli.com
  • Support: support@dinkli.com
  • App: Settings → Support → Privacy Policy

State-Specific Rights

California Residents (CCPA)

  • Right to know what data we collect
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of data sale (we don't sell data)
  • Contact: privacy@dinkli.com

European Users (GDPR) - If Applicable

  • Right to access, rectify, and erase data
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Contact: privacy@dinkli.com

By using Dinkli, you agree to this privacy policy.
