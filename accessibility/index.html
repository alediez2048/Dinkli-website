Accessibility Statement

Last Updated: December 3, 2025

Our Commitment

Dinkli is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards to our mobile application and website.

Accessibility Features

The Dinkli app is designed with inclusivity in mind. We support the following accessibility features native to iOS:

Contrast & Color

We use high-contrast color combinations (like black on green or white) to ensure content is distinguishable without relying solely on color.

Reduced Motion

We respect your system's "Reduce Motion" settings to minimize animations that might cause discomfort.

Standards & Conformance

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at Level AA standards.

Our ongoing efforts include:

  • Testing with screen readers (VoiceOver)
  • Verifying color contrast ratios
  • Ensuring touch targets are at least 44x44 points
  • Providing clear labels for all interactive elements
  • Supporting system-wide accessibility settings

Feedback & Assistance

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of Dinkli. If you encounter any accessibility barriers or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us:

  • Subject Line: Accessibility Feedback

We try to respond to feedback within 2 business days.
