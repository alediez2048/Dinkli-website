Frequently Asked Questions

Account & Registration

How do I create an account?
Tap "Create Account," enter your email and password, verify your email, and complete your profile.
I didn't receive my verification email. What should I do?
Check your spam folder. If still not there, tap "Resend Email" on the verification screen.

Booking Games

How do I book a game?
Browse games in the Explore tab, tap a game to view details, and tap "Reserve Spot" to book.
What payment options are available?
We accept all major credit cards through Stripe. Pay per game ($10) or get a monthly membership ($50).
How do I cancel a booking?
Go to the game details and tap "Cancel Registration." Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours before game time.

Membership

What's the difference between Drop-in and Monthly membership?
  • Drop-in: $10 per game, pay as you play
  • Monthly: $50/month, unlimited games
How do I cancel my monthly membership?
Go to Profile ‚Üí Membership ‚Üí Manage Subscription ‚Üí Cancel.

Technical Issues

The app is crashing. What should I do?
Close and restart the app, check for updates, restart your device, or contact support@dinkli.com.
I can't see any games.
Check your internet connection, pull down to refresh, or contact support@dinkli.com.
